1. Purpose and capabilities
- D3.js provides low-level SVG plotting primitives and utilities to bind data to DOM/SVG and compute visual encodings.
- Promotes building custom visualizations rather than providing high-level chart components.

2. Data-driven approach and workflow notes
- Connects to data sources, files, and databases; enables live data-driven updates.
- Designed for rapid iteration, versioning, and collaborative exploration according to site copy.

3. Typical usage patterns and integration notes
- Use D3 for direct DOM/SVG manipulation, scales, axes, shapes, and data transforms.
- Often combined with higher-level grammars (e.g., Vega-Lite) when authoring concise specs is desired.

4. Supplementary details and attribution
- Source promotional text emphasizes instant connection to data, team collaboration, and fast development workflows.
