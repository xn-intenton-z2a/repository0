String Hamming Distance

Calculate the Hamming distance between two strings of equal length (number of positions where characters differ).

Click "Calculate" to see results
Example: hammingDistance("karolin", "kathrin") → 3

Bit Hamming Distance

Calculate the Hamming distance between two non-negative integers (count of differing bits).

Click "Calculate" to see results
Example: hammingDistanceBits(1, 4) → 2 (binary: 001 vs 100)
